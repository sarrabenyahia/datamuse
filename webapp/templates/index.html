What's Datamuse?

In order to support artistic creation and make contemporary art accessible to all, the City of Paris has a policy of commissioning works of art for the public space. In the same vein, this web application aims to support the city of Paris' culture and help in free information sharing.

Datamuse is the Shazam for cultural and artistic activities. This recommandation system was created for lovers of contemporary art, for gallery owners and other professionals in the sector and for travellers who are planning a trip to the city of art.

How to use it ?


A very clear agenda highlights the exhibitions that are coming to an end, the upcoming openings and the current programming in the galleries.

How to use it? Nothing could be simpler:
- Be tempted by selecting Datamuse,
- Locate all nearby museum, gallery and cultural exhibits,
- Find detailed information about the exhibition (dates and times).

The strengths of Datamuse:
- Search for exhibitions according to your artistic preferences,
- Trust the Datamuse NLP algorithm to make your choice,
- Use your favorite navigation app to get there (Uber, Google Maps).
